About Me

I am a dedicated student currently majoring in Electrical Engineering Engineering and minoring in Software engineering at Clarkson University as an Ignite Presidential Fellow.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I have also developed a diverse set of skills in graphic design and video production. I have a keen eye for design, and I am able to use my skills to develop visually appealing materials and videos to promote events, clubs and projects using my expertise in graphic design software such as Photoshop and Illustrator and video editing software such as Adobe After Effects and Adobe Premiere.

I am also highly motivated to give back to the community by participating in outreach programs, mentoring underprivileged students, and volunteering in various technical and non-technical events. I am committed to using my skills and knowledge to make a positive impact.
